Prompt



Make a selection by dragging the mouse. Then create, inpaint, or copy the selection.

You can also drag and drop an image from your filesystem onto the canvas.

When you drag/drop, or copy a selection, you will get a "Stamp" which is not initially on the canvas (notice the colorful border). You can drag it around and stamp it onto the canvas.

You can use the erase tool by dragging the mouse over the canvas while holding the COMMAND key.
